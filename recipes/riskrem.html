Norwegian Riskrem

Description

This traditional Norwegian Christmas dessert is one everyone can enjoy. This fluffy, creamy dessert is popular around the table during Christmas.

Ingredients

Topping sauce

  • Juice concentrate and water to make roughly 3 cups
  • 10 teaspoons corn starch (3 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon)

Rice Pudding

  • 4 cups rice porridge leftovers
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 half-pint cartons (or 2 cups altogether) heavy whipping cream
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar (or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract)

Steps

Topping sauce

  1. Prepare the topping sauce by whisking together juice and cornstarch until smooth and no lumps remain.
  2. Bring to a boil while stirring, until thickened.
  3. Cool while covered, until well chilled.
  4. Beat again with a wire whisk if needed, until smooth.

Rice pudding

  1. Mix together cold rice porridge leftovers and milke. Set aside.
  2. Beat heavy whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold whipping cream into porridge until blended together, careful not to beat air out of the whipped cream.
  4. Serve with the topping sauce on the side.
